# OmniCapture Plugin Status

## Implemented Capabilities
- **Cubemap rig generation** – `AOmniCaptureRigActor` spawns ±X/±Y/±Z scene capture components, configures 90° FOV render targets, and applies stereo IPD offsets before capturing each face every tick.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureRigActor.cpp†L13-L196】
- **RDG-based equirectangular conversion** – `FOmniCaptureEquirectConverter` drives dedicated compute shaders to stitch the six (or twelve) cubemap faces, emit NV12/P010 plane textures, fence the GPU work, and read back 16-bit image data for PNG export and preview updates.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureEquirectConverter.cpp†L17-L209】
- **World subsystem orchestration** – `UOmniCaptureSubsystem` validates the environment, applies NVENC fallbacks, spawns the rig, preview, and tick actors, pushes frames through the ring buffer, and routes them to PNG or NVENC writers while tracking ring-buffer/audio stats for the editor panel.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureSubsystem.cpp†L41-L366】
- **Background ring buffer** – `FOmniCaptureRingBuffer` runs an FRunnable worker that drains a multi-producer queue with drop-or-block policies to keep high-resolution captures from stalling the render thread.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureRingBuffer.cpp†L8-L213】
- **Audio submix capture** – `FOmniCaptureAudioRecorder` listens to an AudioMixer submix, timestamps PCM16 packets, maintains drift counters, and exports a WAV alongside per-frame packet queues for synchronization during muxing.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureAudioRecorder.cpp†L19-L229】
- **NVENC and PNG outputs** – The NVENC shim initializes AVEncoder on Win64, accepts zero-copy textures or plane arrays, and writes Annex-B bitstreams, while the PNG writer pushes 16-bit-capable frames through `ImageWriteQueue` for lossless output.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureNVENCEncoder.cpp†L57-L214】【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCapturePNGWriter.cpp†L17-L56】
- **Preview and muxing utilities** – The preview actor mirrors equirect frames onto a plane mesh, and the muxer records manifests plus optional FFmpeg commands to package PNG+WAV or NVENC outputs with spherical metadata and faststart/CFR options.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCapturePreviewActor.cpp†L12-L101】【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureMuxer.cpp†L12-L264】
- **Editor tooling** – The `OmniCaptureEditor` module registers a Level Editor toolbar button and dockable panel that hosts capture settings, Start/Stop/Pause/Resume controls, a one-click panoramic still capture button, live frame-rate/ring-buffer telemetry, audio drift readouts, warning lists, and an “Open Output” shortcut sourced from the subsystem diagnostics.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCaptureEditor/Private/OmniCaptureEditorModule.cpp†L14-L96】【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCaptureEditor/Private/SOmniCaptureControlPanel.cpp†L75-L404】
- **Runtime diagnostics & segmentation** – `UOmniCaptureSubsystem` tracks frame-rate, disk space, and dropped frames, exposes pause/resume toggles to the editor, and supports duration/size-based segment rotation with per-segment audio/video manifests for muxing.【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureSubsystem.cpp†L60-L221】【F:Plugins/OmniCapture/Source/OmniCapture/Private/OmniCaptureSubsystem.cpp†L560-L873】
